About

Company Overview

TRADITIONAL ELITE LIMITED is a registered company in Nigeria under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria (Companies and Allied Matter Act 1990) with RC: 1324622. TRADITIONAL ELITE LIMITED is a growing indigenous engineering contracting company with a background and experience of technical staff in the construction, engineering, and architectural designs and contracting sector of Nigeria's economy.

TRADITIONAL ELITE LIMITED has steadily developed into a group with interests in civil engineering and building construction, project management, and General contracts. We focus on the design and management of technology resources, with the aim of optimizing the efficiency of manual and automated processes through the utilization of modern Civil Engineering, Building Construction, and General Contracts. We achieve these by integrating these Technologies to position our clients to meet current management and business challenges, and also prepare them for the ever-changing benchmarks and culture of services delivery.

Our team specializes in offering strategic engineering services to organizations in both the public and private sectors. We pride ourselves on our ability to hard work with or without supervision as we crave to achieve the desired result by making sure that all the nitty-gritty of the project is satisfied on time.
